Seattle Bike Bingo Generator

Bicycle Benefits used to create a physical bingo card which was a fun way to encourage exploration of the city by bike. This site generates a bingo card for you based on the list of participating locations (fetched September 2023, but see the Bicycle Benefits site for the most up-to-date list). You can download the raw data from https://www.bicyclebenefits.org/members (Seattle's city id is 3).
